About Moomin Bakery

Moomin Bakery is a themed Japanese Finnish bakery located in Seattle, WA.

We specialize in handcrafted Moomin-themed drinks, sweets, ice creams, cakes, and Finnish waffles and pastries, all made with love with the fines ingredients.

How we began

The story of Moomin Bakery begins in 2008 with our founder's love for the whimsical TV series, the Moomins, about a family of fairytale Mooomins and their adventures.

Fast forward to 2020, our founder, Minh, joyfully combined her loved for baked goods and the nostalgic series into one with the launch of Moomin Bakery in her home city, Seattle. We are devoted to not only making delicious baked goods and cafe drinks, but also to providing an unique and adorable healing experience for our customers.
